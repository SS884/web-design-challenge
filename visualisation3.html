Cloudiness

Cloudiness has been shown to be at its lowest in the 0 to 40 latitude range, with highest amount of cloud being above the 40 latitude. This could be due to being closer to the equator and causing more moisture in the air which once cooled in the higher atmosphere will turn to cold.
